Audience

Who are the target customers?

  • Adventurous Travelers: Those who crave excitement and are actively seeking thrilling outdoor activities as a part of their travel experiences. Example: Adventure-seeking tourists looking for unique experiences during their vacation.
  • Outdoor Enthusiasts: Nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts who appreciate the beauty of rivers and want to explore the wilderness from a unique perspective. Example: Hikers, campers, and kayakers interested in expanding their outdoor activities.
