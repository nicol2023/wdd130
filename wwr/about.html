Rafting Travel

At Rafting Travel, our purpose is to lead you on exhilarating rafting adventures, delivering the excitement of adrenaline-pumping fun, awe-inspiring landscapes, and indelible memories. Our mission is to serve as your go-to hub for all things rafting, offering valuable insights, booking opportunities, and a community of kindred spirits. We aspire to ignite your passion for unforgettable rafting experiences, always emphasizing safety, environmental responsibility, and the pure joy of outdoor exploration.

History

Founded in 1995, Rafting Travel Co. has a rich history of providing thrilling rafting experiences. From our humble beginnings as a small group of outdoor enthusiasts, we've grown to become a leading provider of white-water rafting adventures in the scenic rivers of the Pacific Northwest. Our journey has been marked by a passion for exploration, a commitment to safety, and a dedication to sharing the beauty of nature with adventurers from around the world.
